GOT QUESTIONS? WE ARE HERE FOR YOU.

What is included in the rent? −
All plans include utilities, Wi-Fi, weekly cleaning, and access to communal spaces. Higher plans add concierge, gym, rooftop, and event access.
What is the minimum stay?+
Minimum 1 month. We offer month-to-month and discounts for 3, 6, or 12-month commitments. Long stays come with significant savings.
Can I bring a pet?+
Many of our units are pet-friendly. Filter by "Pets allowed" when browsing to find the right space for you and your companion.
Is there a co-working space?+
Our co-working space is open 24/7 to all members. Includes private call booths, fast Wi-Fi, and a coffee bar on-site.
How do I book a flat?+
Browse plans above, pick your tier, fill in the form, and our team will contact you within 24 hours.
